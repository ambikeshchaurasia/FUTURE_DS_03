# 📊 Bank Marketing Campaign Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview

This project presents an interactive Power BI dashboard built using the Bank Marketing Campaign dataset. The objective is to analyze customer subscription behavior, evaluate campaign effectiveness, and identify factors influencing term deposit subscriptions.

The dashboard helps stakeholders understand customer demographics, campaign performance, contact methods, and customer engagement to support better marketing decisions.

---

## 🎯 Business Problem

Banks conduct marketing campaigns to encourage customers to subscribe to term deposits. However, not every campaign converts successfully.

This dashboard answers key business questions:

- Which customer groups are more likely to subscribe?
- Which contact method performs better?
- How does campaign frequency affect conversion?
- What customer characteristics influence subscriptions?
- Which months show better campaign performance?

## 📊 Key Insights

- Customers contacted via **Cellular** showed higher subscription rates compared to Telephone and Unknown contact types.
- Customers with longer call durations were more likely to subscribe to a term deposit.
- Customers with higher account balances demonstrated better conversion rates.
- Campaign performance varied across months, indicating seasonality in customer engagement.
- Education and occupation influence customer subscription behavior.
- Customers without housing loans showed relatively better subscription rates.

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations

- Increase campaigns through Cellular contact channels.
- Focus on customer segments with higher balances.
- Optimize campaign timing based on high-performing months.
- Personalize campaigns for different education and job categories.
- Improve call quality and engagement to increase average call duration.
- Reduce excessive repeated campaign contacts to avoid customer fatigue.
